# Space Tourism

Book your flight to the stars. **Space Tourism** is a space-flight booking platform
where you can browse otherworldly destinations, reserve seats on scheduled launches,
pay securely, and manage your trips — all wrapped in an immersive, cinematic interface.

It began as the [Frontend Mentor "Space tourism" multipage UI](https://www.frontendmentor.io/challenges/space-tourism-multipage-website-gRWj1URZ3)
and grew into a complete product with real accounts, live seat inventory, payments,
and refunds.

## What you can do

- **Explore destinations** — discover the Moon, Mars, Europa, and Titan, each with its
  own travel time, distance, and story.
- **See real launches** — every destination has scheduled departures with live seat
  availability and per-seat pricing.
- **Create an account** — sign up with email and password, or continue with Google.
- **Book a trip** — pick a launch, choose how many seats, name your passengers, and
  pay securely through Stripe Checkout. Seats are held while you pay and confirmed the
  moment payment succeeds.
- **Never get double-booked** — seat reservation is guaranteed: a launch can never be
  sold beyond its capacity, even under a rush of simultaneous bookings.
- **Manage your account** — view your profile and a full history of your trips,
  including cancelled and refunded ones.
- **Cancel and get refunded** — cancel an upcoming trip and your payment is refunded
  automatically, with the seats returned to inventory.
- **Confirmation by email** — receive a confirmation once your booking is paid.

## For staff

Administrators get a dedicated panel to manage the catalog and operations:

- Create, edit, and cancel destinations and launches.
- Review every booking and issue refunds.
- See a stats dashboard — revenue, seats sold, and the most popular destinations.

## Try it

Pay with the Stripe **test card** `4242 4242 4242 4242` (any future expiry, any CVC) —
no real money moves.
